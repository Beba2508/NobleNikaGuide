About Villa

Elegant, spacious, and designed for unforgettable relaxation in the hills above Opatija.

Villa Nika accommodates up to 8 guests across 4 luxurious bedrooms, each with an ensuite bathroom, balcony, and Smart TV. The villa spans over 450 m², offering a bright living area, infinity pool, sauna, jacuzzi, and panoramic sea views.

  • 4 king-size bedrooms (180x200cm)
  • Sea-view living room with terrace and infinity pool
  • Fully equipped modern kitchen (oven, coffee machine, dishwasher)
  • Private sauna, jacuzzi, gym, and recreation area
  • High-speed Wi-Fi, air conditioning, and floor heating
  • Private parking for 4 cars within gated courtyard

Comfort & Climate

All rooms have air conditioners. Use the same mode in all (Cooling or Heating). In the living room, control is via thermostat next to the TV. Bathroom heating: towel warmers (switch at the bottom). Terrace fan is controlled with a white remote (on dining shelves).
Power button (far right) to switch on/off. Wave symbol activates anti-fog. V/T MEM adjusts clock and memory settings. Upper section shows time/date, middle displays temperature.

SPA & Recreation

Located in the basement. Press upper power button to start, use middle button for modes/light. Adjust temperature and time using the rotary control. Always shower before and stay hydrated. Limit sessions to 15 minutes.
Infinity pool and jacuzzi on terrace. Automatic cleaning system. Please shower before use and supervise children at all times.
Basement features stationary bike, ping-pong table, and darts. Sauna and shower area behind glass partition.
